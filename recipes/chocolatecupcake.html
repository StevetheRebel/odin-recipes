Dark Chocolate Cupcake

dark chocolate cupcake

  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs (slightly beaten)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 85g unsweatened chocolate
  • 1/8 tsp orange oil
  • 2/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp vanilla extract

Preheat oven 180℃, pepare pan with baking cups


In mixer bowl. cream the butter and sugar. Add eggs, milk, chocolate, vanilla and orange oil, mixing after each addition. Add flour, baking powder and salt; mix just until blended. Fill each baking liner 1/3 full.


Bake 20 - 30 minutes or until toothpick inserted in middle come out clean. Cool in pan on cooling rach 8 minutes. Remove cupcakes from pan. Cool completely before icing.

12 cupcakes
