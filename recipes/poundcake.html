Pound Cake

classic pound cake

  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla Extract
  • 2 2/3 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 cups Butter
  • 1 3/4 cups Granulated Sugar
  • 6 Eggs

Preheat oven 180℃, generously grease inside of flutted tube pan and coat with flour


In mediumbowl, stir together flour, baking powder and salt.


In large bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after addiion. Add vanilla and beat until until well combind. Add flour mixture and beat on low speed for 1 minute, scraping ottom and sides of bowls as needed.Pour into prepared pan.


Bake 50 - 55 minutes or or until toothpic inserted in center of cake comes out clean. Cool in pan on cooling grid for 15 minutes. Loosen sides of cake and remove from pan; cool completely on grid.

Serving: 12 - 16 people
