Red Velvet Cake

dark chocolate cupcake

  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 2 tbsp Cocoa
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1 1/2 cups Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Red Icing Color
  • 1 cup Buttermilk
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tbsp White Vinegar

Preheat oven 180℃. Spray two 9" pans with vegetable pan spray.


In a medium bowl, stir together flour, cocoa and salt; set aside. In large bowl, beat butter, suger and vanilla until light and fluffy. Add eggs and red icing color, mix well. Add flour micture alternately with buttermilk to butter mixture, beating until well blended. Stir baking soda into vinegar and fold carefully into batter. Pour batter into prepared pans.


Bake 30 - 35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 10 minutes, remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely

Serving: 10 - 15 people
